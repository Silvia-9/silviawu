Hello! My name is Silvia Wu (Chinese name: Shih-hsuan Wu). I'm originally from Taiwan and a passionate linguist, fluent in Chinese, English, and French.

My professional journey spans over a decade, encompassing diverse roles that have honed my expertise in global business. I've spent two years in international sales, three years as an international business development manager, two years contributing to new product development, and five years specializing in supply chain and logistics management. These experiences have given me a comprehensive understanding of international commerce and operations.

Currently, I'm passionate about software development and data analysis, combining my international business background with modern technology to create innovative solutions. I enjoy working with JavaScript, Python, and various web development frameworks.

When I'm not diving into the world of business or coding, I love to explore! Traveling, hiking, and discovering new countries are some of my favorite ways to spend my free time, as they constantly broaden my perspective and inspire new ideas.
